## Example 8: Line-prefix stripping.
## Diagram expressions can be written as comments in various programming languages.
## Leading //, --, or # (but NOT ##) are stripped as language prefixes.
## No trailing space after the prefix is required.
## A line containing only //, --, or # also acts as the section separator.
## ## (with or without a space) is the box-diagram comment marker.

//A := box("Input")
//B := r-box("Process")
//C := box("Output")
--D := text("Source")
--E := text("Sink")   ## E is plain text, -- is the language prefix here
//
//D -> A -> B -> C -> E
