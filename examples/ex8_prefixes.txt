## Example 8: Line-prefix stripping.
## Diagram expressions can be written as comments in various programming languages.
## Leading //, --, or # (followed by whitespace) are stripped as language prefixes.
## ## (with or without a space) starts a box-diagram comment to end of line.

// B := r-box("Process")
